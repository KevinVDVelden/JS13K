Welcome to Visible & Co!

Here at Visible & Co we pride ourselves on using the best guards and the best Thievery Deterrent Systems (TDS©) money can buy!
Unfortunately the client you have been assigned to did not pay for that tier of support so you'll have to make do.

As you may have heard congress has recently passed a law making the maximum prison sentences for thievery limited only by how many items the would be thief has on them when they are caught.
Our subsidiary Prison Inc is very happy with this news, of course it is illegal for us to try to lure thieves in to get more money and as such we recommend you catch intruders as soon as possible!
Longer sentences would however look good on your monthly review, so if it does happen that a thief penetrates far in to our defenses before getting caught, well, that's just the nature of things.

Here are the important things you must recognise and know to be an efficient thief catcher:

A guard.

These stallwart defenders of the law have the best training that 4 evenings of nightschooling can supply!

When an alarm is triggered they will go over to it to check it out. Alternatively you can send them to a place you choose by using the "Alert" button and clicking on a tile.
If the guard is close enough they will check it, in both cases if nothing happens the guard will return to their station after a short while.

If they stand next to a thief that thief will be caught and teleported to lockup.

A thief!

These scoundrels and thrill seekers want nothing more than to steal your (clients) stuff.

Mostly bored people with some mail order catalogue gadgets they will enter from one of the entrances and loot stuff until they get bored.
The best way to keep your "guests" entertained is via the use of some TDS© devices as explained below.

When bored or scared they'll start heading towards the exits, catch them before they leave!

Boredom increases when nothing interesting happens to a thief, certain things cause a thief to become more afraid, this will slowly decay over time.

Loot!

Thiefs will try to steal this, if they escape with the loot it is lost forever.
If a guard captures a thief carrying loot they will bring the loot back to it's proper place.

Entrance

Thieves will enter from here, looking for loot.

Exit

Thieves will exit from here, they will be disabled for a while if an Alarm DTS© goes off nearby.

Alarm TDS©

All TDS© tiles can be triggered in one of 2 ways. A thief stepping on it, or a connecting alarm going off.

All triggered devices will affect the original victim, so an alarm will call guards to the originally triggered tile.

After use, a TDS© becomes momentarily disabled.

Freeze TDS©

When triggered, this will momentarily freeze the unlucky victim, making them easy targets for guards.

This will slightly decrease a thief's boredom

Zap TDS©

When triggered, this will cause a big jolt to the victim, making them feel alive and slightly singed.

This will greatly decrease a thief's boredom, but will also scare them!
